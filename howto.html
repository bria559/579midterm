TIips on how to keep your plants healthy

Always check your plants for diseases and pests before purchasing them. If there are diseases or pests, keep these plants isolated from other healthy plants so the healthy plants are not infected as well.
If you want to plant seeds for your houseplants, buy organic seeds from a local business. You can also propogate plants yourself.
To keep your plants growing, you can repot them every 6 months to a year.
Each plant requires a different amount of sunlight and water. Some plants, like baby tears, require water once a week and low light. Other plants require water more frequently or more sunlight. Research to learn your plants' specific needs so they can thrive!
